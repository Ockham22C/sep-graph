SEP: Fall 2018

Below is a collection of community graphs from the fall 2018 archive of the SEP. You can click through to see the different communities, zoom, pan, and hover on nodes to see names.

The communities are unlabeled because they are not based on any domain knowledge. However, you may be able to infer the subfields that are represented in these communities.
